    Qaidam black goji berry is a magical biological resource, it rich in nutrition ingredients and medicine care active ingredients. According the research, the black goji berry contain carotene, thiamine, riboflavin, nicotinic acid, ascorbic acid, β-sitosterol, linoleic acid , zeaxanthine, betaine, wintercherry haematochrome and etc. Mineral substance and trace element Ca, Fe, P,Cu, Zn contain are also very high .The part of water-solubility in the fruit rich in goji activity polysaccharide, it is the main effective element of medical care and health care. The amino acid in the black goji berry, most of them is free amino acid, is much higher than goji berry, can be fully absorbed by people.

    Black goji berry use as medicine same as Ningxia goji berry, has the function of keep liver, improve eyesight, resist fatigue, beauty and resist tumour etc. Tibetan medicine called the black goji berry “pangma”, the record according to take the mature fruit for the Tibetan medicine, can cure the heart disease, cardiopyretic disease, abnormal menstruation, menopause and etc. < Uyghur medicine scriptures> record that Uyghur doctors use the black goji berry and the velamen to cure urethral calculus, tinea, bleeding gums and etc. People use it to improve eyesight, lower blood pressure and nourishing.

    Modern Pharmacology research proofs that the pigment in the black goji berry has good antioxidant activity on the mouse serum, organelle, cell and tissue, polyphenol contain can be reach to 73.8%, indicate its health effects on living organisms.
